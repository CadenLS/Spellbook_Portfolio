Echolith (Current Project)

Echolith is a spell-based VR game where players can verbally declare the spell they wish to cast and then unleash it with immersive motion controls. The game features a wide variety of spells and spell types, encouraging experimentation and creativity. Whether you want to hurl fireballs or summon monsters, Echolith lets you experience magic in a hands-on, interactive way.

Physics Game

This Physics Game lets users place balls with different masses and adjust physics settings like gravity and time step (which affects simulation speed and accuracy). It’s a sandbox for exploring game physics, created to deepen and share my interest in the subject.

Echolon (Personal Project)

Echolon is a personal project I'm developing that combines elements of soulslike and gacha games—two genres I really enjoy. The game will feature challenging, soulslike combat alongside a gacha system for collecting unique characters, weapons, and abilities. Each character will have their own playstyle and abilities, offering lots of variety and replayability. I'm excited to bring together deep combat and the thrill of collection in one experience.
