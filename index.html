Lex Talionis A

Lex Talionis A, the law of retaliation, whereby a punishment resembles the offense committed. As Aliens you must fight your way through astronauts, drones and bosses in order to enact your revenge on the humans for their hostile takeover. Every alien life is sacred, and in this team based couch co-op experience you will have to place your trust in your companions to not use all your lives. There are four aliens to choose from with abilities ranging from a golf club to summoning a mammoth. But no matter who you choose you mustn't give up on your assault..THIS..IS..WAR!
